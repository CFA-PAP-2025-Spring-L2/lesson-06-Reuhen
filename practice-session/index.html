Animal Aristocracy

Dove

The courtier dove fluffed his feathers, bowed to a daisy, and declared it the queen of spring with all the pomp of a feathered diplomat.

Royal Fox

The royal fox donned a velvet cape, sipped tea from an acorn cup, and issued royal decrees to a council of very confused squirrels.

General Snout

General Snout, with a monocle and a top hat, led his army of ants into battle against the invading army of ladybugs.

Duke Dog

The duke dog, with a crown of daisies, barked orders to his loyal subjects: a parade of squirrels and a marching band of frogs.

Mens Parlor

The mens parlor was a gathering of the most distinguished animals, gambling and discussing the latest gossip about the royal hedgehog.

Queen Cat

The queen cat, with a jeweled collar, lounged on her throne of pillows, plotting her next move in the game of thrones.
